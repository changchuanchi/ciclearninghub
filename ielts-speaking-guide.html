01

考试概览

Exam Overview

雅思口语测试为考官与考生一对一的面试，全程录音，时长约 11–14 分钟，分为三个部分，以 0–9 分制按四项标准各 25% 计算。口语成绩对院校录取和移民申请均有独立要求。

考试三部分结构

部分时长说明
Part 14–5 min日常问答：考官询问个人信息、生活习惯和兴趣爱好等熟悉话题，约 10–12 个问题，以简短回答为主。
Part 23–4 min个人陈述（Long Turn）：考生拿到题卡后有 1 分钟准备，须连续发言 1–2 分钟。结束后考官可追问 1–2 个问题。
Part 34–5 min深度讨论：考官就 Part 2 话题延伸，提出更抽象或具社会性的问题，考查分析、论证和推理能力。

25%

25%

25%
Pronunciation
发音

1. 流利与连贯 Fluency & Coherence

  • 能持续说话，无需过多停顿或自我修正
  • 使用连接词和语篇标记词（discourse markers）使表达有条理
  • 回答切题、逻辑清晰，不出现无关跳跃
流利不等于快速！自然的停顿和思考是允许的；过度追求语速反而会导致错误增加，影响其他维度评分。

2. 词汇资源 Lexical Resource

  • 词汇范围广泛，能精准选词表达意义
  • 能以换句话说（paraphrase）弥补词汇不足
  • 使用搭配词（collocations）、习惯用语（idioms）
  • 偶有错误但不影响理解
鼓励学生使用同义替换，如 happy → thrilled / content / overjoyed；学习常见搭配，如 make a decision 而非 do a decision。

3. 语法范围与准确度 Grammatical Range & Accuracy

  • 能灵活使用多种句型（简单句、复合句、条件句、被动语态等）
  • 大多数情况下能准确运用语法，偶有小错
  • 能保持时态的一致性
鼓励学生刻意加入复杂句型，如条件句（If I had the chance, I would...）、定语从句（The place that I love most...）等。

4. 发音 Pronunciation

  • 发音清晰，听者可轻松理解
  • 正确使用语调（intonation）、重音（word/sentence stress）
  • 连读（connected speech）自然
  • 带有母语口音可被接受，但不应影响理解
发音标准并非要求英音或美音，而是"可理解性"（intelligibility）。重点训练重音、语调起伏和连读技巧。

高分回答范例

Do you work or are you a student?
⭐ 普通回答（约 5–6 分）
"I'm a student. I study English at university."
🏆 高分回答（7–8 分）
"I'm currently in my second year studying English linguistics at university. It's a fascinating field because it bridges language theory and real-world communication, which I find both intellectually stimulating and practically useful."
得分要点：提供具体信息（年级/专业）→ 延伸说明原因 → 加入个人感受。避免只答 Yes/No 或单句回应。
What do you like about your hometown?
⭐ 普通回答
"My hometown is nice. There are many good restaurants."
🏆 高分回答
"What I love most about my hometown is its vibrant street food scene. There's a night market just five minutes from my house, and the variety is incredible — from traditional local desserts to international cuisines. Beyond food, there's a strong sense of community, which makes me feel grounded whenever I go back."
得分要点：使用 "What I love most..." 强调重点；加入具体细节；使用高分词汇（vibrant, grounded）；复句与列举并用。
Do you prefer spending time indoors or outdoors?
🏆 高分回答
"Honestly, it really depends on my mood and the season. On weekends I tend to gravitate towards outdoor activities like cycling or visiting parks — fresh air genuinely helps me de-stress after a hectic week. But during the hot season, I'd much rather stay indoors with the air-conditioning on and curl up with a good book."
得分要点："It depends..." 展示复杂思维；条件句结构；高分词汇（gravitate towards, de-stress, hectic）；自然对比结构。

高分回答范例

Describe a place you enjoy visiting. [地点类]
You should say: where it is · how often you go there · what you do there · and explain why you like visiting this place.
🏆 高分回答示范
"The place I'd like to talk about is a small riverside café in Bangkok, located along the Chao Phraya River. I first discovered it almost by accident while exploring a different neighbourhood, and I've been returning ever since — probably once a month.

What I love about it is that it's tucked away from the tourist crowds, so there's a peaceful, almost timeless quality to it. Whenever I visit, I grab a coffee, find a corner seat by the window, and simply watch the river boats drifting past. Sometimes I bring a book; other times I just sit and think.

The reason I keep going back is that it genuinely feels like an escape. Bangkok can be overwhelming — the noise, the traffic, the relentless pace — and that café gives me the chance to step back and recharge. The combination of the view, the smell of coffee, and the relaxed atmosphere is almost therapeutic. It's become a kind of ritual for me."
得分要点：
流利连贯：段落自然衔接，使用 ever since / other times / The reason... 等连接词
词汇资源：tucked away · timeless quality · therapeutic · overwhelming · ritual
语法范围：复合句（while exploring）、对比结构（sometimes / other times）、名词子句
发音提示：建议教师示范 therapeutic 及 tucked away 的正确重音与语调
Describe a person who has had a great influence on you. [人物类]
You should say: who this person is · how you know them · what this person does or did · and explain why this person has influenced you.
🏆 高分回答示范
"The person I'd like to describe is my secondary school English teacher. I met her when I was about fourteen, at a point when I was genuinely struggling with the subject — not just the grammar, but the confidence to actually use the language.

What made her exceptional was her patience and her ability to make every student feel valued. She had a remarkable talent for turning dry vocabulary exercises into something almost like storytelling — she'd create vivid scenarios where we actually wanted to participate, rather than just memorise.

The reason she had such a profound influence on me is that she fundamentally changed how I approach learning. Before her class, I treated English as an obstacle. Afterwards, I began to see it as a tool — a way to access a wider world."
得分要点：
结构清晰：介绍人物 → 描述特质 → 说明影响，三段式架构
高分词汇：remarkable talent · vivid scenarios · profound influence
复杂语法：名词化结构（how I approach learning）、强调句
个人化：具体细节（年龄、当时状态）让回答更真实、更具说服力

高分回答范例

Why do you think people like to travel?
🏆 高分回答
"I think the reasons are quite varied, but if I had to identify the most compelling ones, it comes down to a combination of escapism and personal growth. Travel gives people the chance to step outside the familiar — their routines, their cultural assumptions — and encounter the world from a completely different perspective.

There's also an increasingly prominent social dimension to it. With platforms like Instagram and Xiaohongshu, travel has become a way of constructing and projecting identity. People don't just travel to experience; they travel to document and share.

That said, I think the deeper motivation for most people is still a desire for perspective — to return home feeling slightly different, slightly broader in their understanding of the world."
得分要点："if I had to identify" 展示复杂句型；escapism · perspective · prominent 等高分词汇；多角度分析，并以 "That said" 做转折对比。
How has the way people communicate changed in recent years?
🏆 高分回答
"The transformation has been quite dramatic. A few decades ago, most interpersonal communication was either face-to-face or via phone calls — there was an expectation of real-time engagement. Now, we've shifted towards asynchronous communication: messages, voice notes, social media posts — all of which allow people to engage on their own terms.

What strikes me as particularly significant is the way digital communication has both expanded and, in some ways, diluted human connection. We're technically more reachable than ever, yet there's a growing sense of disconnection — particularly among younger generations who've grown up without the habit of sustained, face-to-face conversation.

The challenge moving forward, I think, will be about intentionality — choosing the right mode of communication rather than simply defaulting to whatever is most convenient."
得分要点：时态控制精确（过去 vs 现在）；asynchronous · diluted · intentionality 等学术词汇；复杂句型；批判性思维。

高分策略与教学建议

Strategies & Teaching Tips

PEEL 延伸回答架构

步骤说明
P – Point（论点）直接表达主要观点
E – Elaborate（展开）进一步解释或说明
E – Example（举例）给出具体例子或个人经历
L – Link back（回扣）回扣主题，加入个人感受或结论

高分词汇与连接词速查

功能例句 / 短语
表达观点As far as I'm concerned / From my perspective / I'd argue that / It's my belief that
延伸说明What's more / Furthermore / In addition to that / Not only that, but...
举例For instance / A case in point would be / Take... as an example
对比转折On the other hand / That said / Having said that / Conversely
强调What strikes me as... / What I find particularly interesting is... / It's worth noting that
结论收尾All things considered / On balance / Ultimately / To sum up
争取思考时间That's an interesting question... / Let me think about that... / It's not something I've considered before, but...

常见失分陷阱

  • 回答过短：只答 Yes/No 或一句话，缺乏延伸
  • 过度使用填充词：um, uh, like 过多，影响流利度评分
  • 背诵答案：考官可识别背稿，且背稿会导致语调平板
  • 词汇重复：同一个词在短时间内大量重复，限制词汇分数
  • Part 2 计时失控：未说满 1 分钟，或在考官打断前已无话可说
  • 句型单一：只用简单句，语法范围分数受限

练习建议

  • 每天用英语自言自语 5–10 分钟，训练口语流畅度
  • 录音回放，检查停顿过长、词汇重复等问题
  • Part 2 题卡练习：用计时器限时 1 分钟准备、2 分钟发言
  • 每周学习 5 个搭配词（collocations），并练习造句
  • 模拟 Part 3 讨论：选取社会议题（科技/教育/环境），练习多角度回答
可利用 AI 对话工具模拟考官提问，为学生提供即时词汇与语法反馈，作为正式模拟考前的辅助练习。

考场策略与应对语句

Exam Strategies & Useful Phrases

实用考场应对语句

情境建议语句
没听清楚时"Could you please repeat the question?" / "I'm sorry, could you say that again?"
需要思考时"That's an interesting question. Let me think about that..." / "Off the top of my head, I'd say..."
话题不熟悉时"I don't have much personal experience with this, but I imagine..." / "It's not something I've considered before, but I'd suppose..."
修正自己时"What I mean to say is..." / "Let me rephrase that..."
补充观点时"And another thing worth mentioning is..." / "Beyond that, I think..."

考前一周准备清单

  • 复习并练习 Part 1 高频话题（家庭、工作、科技、兴趣爱好）
  • 练习至少 10 张 Part 2 题卡，计时发言
  • 熟练至少 3 套 Part 3 观点延伸架构
  • 录音自评：检查词汇多样性、句型变化、发音清晰度
  • 确认考场地点，携带有效身份证件
  • 考试当天不要过度紧张：流利比完美更重要
